MamáMind Account & Data Deletion

We understand and respect your right to data privacy. You can request the permanent deletion of your MamáMind account and all associated data from our servers.

To begin the process, please click the button below. This will open your email client with a pre-filled message. Simply send the email from the address associated with your MamáMind account.

Request Data Deletion

What will be deleted?

Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete the following data from our servers:

  • Your email address
  • Your subscription status and history

Please note: As stated in our Privacy Policy, your personal journal entries and mood logs are stored only on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not affected by this server data deletion request. To delete this local data, you must uninstall the MamáMind app from your device.
